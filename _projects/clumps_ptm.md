An algorithm for identifying statistically significant 3D clusters of post-translational modifications (PTMs) on protein structures. CLUMPS-PTM maps phosphorylation, acetylation, ubiquitination, and other modification sites onto 3D protein structures to detect spatial enrichment, helping to nominate functionally important regulatory hotspots. It was applied across the CPTAC pan-cancer cohorts in {% cite geffen2023ptm %}, and used to identify PTM-associated structural clusters in the CPTAC lung adenocarcinoma cohort in {% cite satpathy2025lung %}.
