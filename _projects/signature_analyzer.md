A fast, GPU-enabled implementation of Bayesian non-negative matrix factorization (NMF) for extracting mutational and gene-expression signatures from genomic data. SignatureAnalyzer is used across several large-scale cancer genomics efforts, including the PCAWG and CPTAC pan-cancer studies, to discover and assign *de novo* mutational signatures without specifying the number of signatures in advance, as described in {% cite taylorweiner2019scaling %}.
